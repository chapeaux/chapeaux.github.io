We're excited to launch the new Chapeaux Project website. This site serves as the central hub for everything related to Chapeaux — a digital supply chain for web assets.

## What's Here

The new site brings together all aspects of the project in one place:

- **[Getting Started](/getting-started/)** — Everything you need to begin working with Chapeaux tools
- **[Projects](/projects/)** — A catalog of all repositories in the Chapeaux ecosystem, organized by our three pillars
- **[Chapellerie](/chapellerie/)** — Our playground for demos, experiments, and testing
- **[About](/about/)** — The philosophy and design principles behind the project

## The Three Pillars

Chapeaux is organized around three concerns — what we call the "3 Ds":

1. **Develop** — Creating enterprise-grade web development tools (Active)
2. **Discover** — Building a central catalog for web assets (In Progress)
3. **Deliver** — Standardizing web asset delivery (Coming Soon)

Each pillar is at a different stage of maturity, and the [Projects](/projects/) page shows the current status of every repository.

## Get Involved

Chapeaux is open source and we welcome contributors. Check out the [Getting Started](/getting-started/) guide or browse the [projects catalog](/projects/) to find something that interests you.
